Aims & Objectives

Together Today for a Successful Tomorrow

Cosmos College of Management and Technology was established in 2001 with the objective of providing quality education in the fields of engineering and management in Nepal, which are essential to excel in the contemporary world. At Cosmos we try to prepare quality engineers, technologists, managers and professionals who will be able to serve the nation. We focus on innovation, leadership and management skills required for the changing work environment. Since its inception in 2001 A.D., We have been producing professionals who can compete successfully in the international job market. Thus, the college has been implementing strategies and plans with its number of qualified and highly professional teaching faculty and staff members to offer education in a cosmopolitan manner. The courses along with their teaching methodology will help its students to experience their studies in a duo of a theoretical and practical way of learning. The method will help students to build their career with a strong foundation from their learning phase to going on the field for job experiences. Cosmos College has been providing courses on bachelor level programs in Information Technology, Civil Engineering, Electronics and Communication, Architecture, BBA and Computer Engineering.

Missions

  • To develop globally competent human capital through career-focused quality education;
  • To provide an educational environment that emphasizes critical thinking and decision making, reflection, and creativity;
  • To provide progressive and accessible opportunities;
  • To prepare our students to be responsible citizens.

Core Values

  • Quality
  • Innovativeness.
  • Professionalism.
  • Integrity.
  • Exposure.

Visions

To be the preferred provider of a diverse and progressive educational environment that will enrich the lives of our students, employees, and community.
